Interests & Hobbies

Gaming

I have interest in gaming. My favourite genre tends to be sandbox, strategy and/or role-playing games. My favourite game is Persona 5 Royal which is a JRPG. I enjoy watching e-sports to see the amazing talent in some games and speedrunning to see how people are able to break games. Gaming is a good way for me to pass time and I especially enjoy when I play with friends as it makes even losing or failing fun.

Music

I enjoy listening to music to calm myself down or just to pass the time. I dont mind listening to any kind of music but I prefer more calming music like jazz or rock. Usually I have music play in the background while doing work or doing an assignment so I don't get bored and music helps me keep my focus.

Coding

Of course, as a software development student, I would enjoy coding. I find it exhilarating to make code and trying to get it to work. This is why I hope to be a back-end developer as I find the code to be reasonable and non-complicated. I am able to do coding assignments in one sitting with my entire focus on it.

News

It also helps when some good news is ever mentioned as it shows that there is some hope in this conceringly horrendous world.

Anime and Shows
